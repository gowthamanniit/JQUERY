jumping statement:
==================
	def: execution statement move to one place to another place.	

	types:
	
	1) break;
	2) continue;

1) break;
	--> only terminate the loop.
2) continue;
	-->skip the below statement in loop.	
	-->move to increment position.
	  (for loop)
	-->move to condition
	  (while or do..while)
